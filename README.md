If you want a day-by-day route through the material, start here:

### Outcome
- Day 1 gives you frontend foundations and JS core mastery.
- Day 2 gives you dynamic client-side app skills and API handling.
- Day 3 gives you full React mastery plus state tooling.
- Day 4 gives you full API-building foundations with databases.
- Day 5 gives you auth, uploads, validation, and app architecture.
- Day 6 gives you DevOps, cloud, testing, and scaling basics.
- Day 7 gives you realtime, Next.js, and production deployment polish.
